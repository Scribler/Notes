Mochi Section

GIF Format

Good for simple animations but the color and transparancy limitations mean there are better options for still images.

JPG Format

JPG images are good for situations where you want to keep file size small but need good color pallet size as well. Are limited by their lack of transparency.

PNG Format

Not good for photos due to their large file size, but since they can use transparancy and don't have color pallet limitations they are excellent for logo's and icons and technical diagrams.

SVG Format

SVG's can be used for all the same things as PNG's and should be used whenever possible due to their unique ability to scale.
