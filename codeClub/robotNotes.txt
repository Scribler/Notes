ARTIE MAX COMMANDS

artie.forward(###) move ###mm forward

artie.left(###) move ###mm left

artie.right(###) move ###mm right

artie.movearc(100,-30) move 100 units, arc at 30 degrees to the left
Note - Values of -90, 0, 90 will error since they will not draw a curve. Use .left, .right respectively

artie.penup() - lift the marker dup

artie.pendown(1) - lower a marker to draw
